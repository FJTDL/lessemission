Electricity providers

Whilst most of the electricity providers in New Zealand generate from renewable sources, some still burn coal, which adds to your carbon footprint. The good news is: Because so many providers generate from renewables such as hydroelectric and geothermal, you can switch really easily and maybe save some money in the process.

As much as 82% of the electricity in NZ is generate from geothermal and hydroelectric electricity, with the remainder most being coal. Do you know where your electricity comes from?
